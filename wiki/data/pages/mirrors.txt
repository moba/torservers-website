====== Tor Mirrors ======

People need alternative ways to download Tor if torproject.org is blocked for them.
===== Torproject.org Mirrors =====

( https://www.torproject.org/getinvolved/mirrors.html.en )

  * https://www.torservers.net/mirrors/torproject.org/
  * https://www.oignon.net/
  * https://www.torproject.us/
  * https://tor.beme-it.de/
  * https://torproj.xpdm.us/
  * https://tor.blingblingsquad.net/
  * https://tor.tomyn.com/
  * https://tor-www.5coluna.com/
  * http://jan25.virtadpt.net/ (Source, executables, browser bundles, all with PGP signatures.)

===== Bittorrent =====

  * All releases: https://www.torservers.net/mirrors/torrents/

If you suspect your Internet connection to be monitored, make sure to turn off [[http://www.bittorrent.org/beps/bep_0022.html|Local Tracker Discovery]] in your BT client (only implemented in a few clients and should already be disabled by default).
