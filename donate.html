Donations

Support us financially

We are a registered charitable non-profit organization based in Germany. All donations will be used in accordance to our statute to advance secure communications. If you donate more than $50 monthly, you are entitled to get your very own Tor exit node: Contact us and we'll set up a node carrying a name of your choice, with a custom info page (if desired)!

Every donation will be listed on our site and accumulated until we can use them to purchase additional bandwidth or another node. Bandwidth stats are published (independently controlled by TorStatus), so you will be able to see what's happening with your money!

We encourage everyone to subscribe to our low volume mailing list and follow us on Twitter.

We offer a broad selection of ways to donate:
PayPal
Paypal accepts all major credit cards and wire transfers in most countries. It also allows you to set up recurring donations.
SWIFT European Bank Transfer
Bank transfers are free within Germany, and many other European banks offer cheap SWIFT transfers. This is the recommended method for Europeans, especially for recurring donations.
Anonymous donations
Liberty Reserve is a Costa Rica based privacy aware payment processor.
UKash and PaySafeCard are prepaid voucher codes you can buy across Europe in various stores and gas stations.
Bitcoin is a "peer to peer cryptocurrency" - see bitcoin.org
We also accept almost any prepaid gift credit cards.
Flattr
Flattr is a convenient social one-click micropayment solution.

No matter which method you use, you can decide if you want your name, nickname and/or website URL listed on our page or stay anonymous. If we don't hear differently from you, we will not publish your name!

Donate via Paypal

For your convenience we provide a PayPal gateway for donations. If possible, use Euro as currency as this will make accounting easier for us; Paypal will automatically convert it to your local currency.
